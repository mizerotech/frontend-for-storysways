Local Stories & PR Services

Mission

To empower unemployed youth, especially journalism graduates, with digital storytelling skills, while providing affordable PR services to local businesses, strengthening local media ecosystem and economic visibility.

Vision

A community where authentic local stories drive global narratives, youth thrive in digital communications careers, and businesses leverage storytelling to attract investment and growth.

Our Core Values

  • Empowerment – We believe in equipping the next generation with the tools to shape their own narratives and careers.
  • Authenticity – We champion genuine storytelling that resonates with truth and integrity.
  • Innovation – We embrace new technologies and creative approaches to communications.
  • Collaboration – We foster partnerships between youth, businesses, and media for collective impact.
  • Sustainability – We build lasting skills and create services that contribute to long-term economic growth.

What We Do

  1. Train Youth
    Offer hands-on training in digital journalism, content creation, social media management, and PR strategies.
  2. Provide PR Services
    Deliver professional, affordable PR services to local SMEs and organizations, helping them reach wider audiences and attract investment.
  3. Bridge the Gap
    Connect trained youth with job opportunities in media and communications, reducing unemployment.
  4. Strengthen Local Media
    Contribute to a vibrant and diverse local media landscape by promoting local content and talent.
